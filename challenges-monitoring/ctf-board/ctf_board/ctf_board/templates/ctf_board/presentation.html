Presentation

After the conferences (see the confs planning here) InSecurity organizes a CTF (Capture The Flag).

If you live in Lyon, we recommand you to come on site to participate (see where the CTF takes place). There will be free drinks/food, more challenges and gifts !
Otherwise you can also participate online (using this website).

In the CTF you will find challenges of different kind, like web, steganography, cryptography, reverse and binaries.
There will be challenges for everyone, beginners or experienced !

The CTF starts the Thursday 07 April at 06:30PM (GMT+2) and ends the Thursday 07 April at 11:59PM (GMT+2).

Some rules

  • It will be a Jeopardy-style CTF
  • The firsts 3 teams to flag a challenges receive bonus points (+33% for the first, +25% for the second then 16% for the third)
  • You should register your team here before the 07/04. A team should contain 1 to 4 participants.
  • Some challenges will only be available on site
  • Any degradation of service may result in disqualification
